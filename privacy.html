Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

VocaPic is a vocabulary learning application that helps you master English through interactive flashcards, audio playlists, and vivid illustrations organized by real-life topics. VocaPic is operated by the VocaPic Team.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile applications and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Note: VocaPic is designed as an offline-first application. Most features work entirely on your device without sending data to external servers.

2. Information We Collect

Stored Locally on Your Device

Data Purpose Shared Externally
Playlist data (created playlists, saved words) App functionality No
Learning preferences (repeat count, pause duration) App functionality No
Audio playback state App functionality No

VocaPic does not send any data to external servers. All content is bundled with the app and works entirely offline.

3. How We Use Your Information

a. Providing and improving the Service

  • Store your playlists and learning progress locally on your device
  • Provide audio playback functionality for vocabulary learning

b. Anonymous information

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not track your behavior within the app
  • We do not use advertising identifiers

4. Third-Party Services

VocaPic does not use any third-party services that collect or process your personal data. All content and functionality is provided locally on your device.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Local storage: SQLite database on your device
  • No cloud storage: VocaPic does not store your data on remote servers
  • No network requests: The app does not transmit any data over the internet
  • No accounts: VocaPic does not require registration or login

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the application

Since we do not store your data on external servers, uninstalling the app permanently removes all associated data.

7. Your Rights

Since VocaPic stores data only on your device and does not maintain user accounts or cloud storage, you have full control over your data:

  • View all your data within the app (playlists, settings)
  • Delete individual playlists or vocabulary items within the app
  • Delete all data by clearing app storage or uninstalling

For any data-related questions, please contact us at vocapic.app@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

VocaPic is suitable for learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app does not require registration and stores data only locally, no personal information is transmitted to our servers.

Parents or guardians with concerns may contact us at vocapic.app@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. If changes are material, we will notify you via the app. Your continued use of VocaPic after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at vocapic.app@gmail.com.
